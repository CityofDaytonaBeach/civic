Exciting Update: Our Public Meeting Livestreams Are Moving!
In the near future, we’ll be transitioning our public meeting livestreams to a brand-new YouTube channel. We’re excited about this change and look forward to sharing all our future content in a fresh, consolidated space.
What Does This Mean for You?
Our public meetings will soon be available on the new channel, where we’ll also organize videos into dedicated playlists for easy access.
You can still watch past meetings on our old channel while we make this transition, so no need to worry about missing anything!

We appreciate your support as we move things over, and we’ll keep you updated as the switch happens. Be sure to subscribe to the new channel, so you don’t miss out on any future video content.
