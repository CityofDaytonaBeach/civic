BECOMING A CANDIDATE Municipal Election Information provided may be subject to Legislative Changes If you are interested in running for office, please contact the City Clerk’s Office at 386- 671- 8023. The City Clerk will meet with you and provide an Election Packet. If you are interested in running for office, please contact the City Clerk’s Office at 386-671-8023. The City Clerk will meet with you and provide an Election Packet. Nominating Petitions Information: City Charter – Subpart A, Article V, Section 19. - “The mode of nomination of all elective officers provided for by this Charter shall be by petition. The name of any elector of the city shall be printed upon the ballot as a candidate for either mayor or c ommissioner, whenever a petition, as hereinafter prescribed, shall have been filed in his or her behalf with the city clerk. For candidates for the office of city commissioner, such petition shall be signed by at least twenty -five (25) of the qualified electors residing in the zone in which said candidate resides, and from which said candidates seek election. For candidates for office of mayor, such petition shall be signed by at least ten (10) of the qualified electors from each of the zones of the city. “Nominating petitions for the office of city commissioner shall be filed only upon forms provided for and supplied by the City Clerk, which said forms shall be provided without charge; provided however, that such nominating forms be given by the City Clerk only to a bona fide candidate who shall first have signed and filed with said Clerk and affidavit indicating a bona fide intention to become a candidate. The signatures to a nominating petition need not all be appended to one paper, but to each separate paper there shall be attached an affidavit of the circulation thereof, stating the number of signers of such paper, and that each signature appended thereto was made in the presence of the affiant and is the genuine signature of the person whose name is pu rports to be. With each signature shall be stated the place of residence of the signer, giving the street and number, of other description sufficient to identify the same.” All questions regarding the nomination process should be directed to the City Clerk. Additional Requirements at the time of Qualifying : Affidavit of Intent (Signed and Notarized) & Nominating Petitions (certified by the Supervisor of Elections). Oath of Candidate (Signed and Notarized - Copy of Voter Registration Card or number). Form 1 Statement of Financial Interests (202 5) **Must be filed electronically with the Florida Commission on Ethics before the end of the qualifying period. A public officer who has filed the public disclosure with the Commission on Ethics before qualifying for office may file a copy of that disclosure or a verification or receipt of electronic filing at the time of qualifying. ** Acceptance of the Nomination Form. Payment of election assessment: 1% of the annual salary, to include expense allowances, must be paid not later than at the time the candidate accepts the nomination and returns his/her qualifying petitions to the City Clerk. The City filing fee must be paid not later than at the time the candidate accepts the nomination for office .
