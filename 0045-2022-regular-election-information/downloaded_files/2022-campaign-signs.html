LJL 03 -16-16 THE CITY OF DAYTONA BEACH DEVELOPMENT AND ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES DEPARTMENT PERMIT & LICENSES DIVISION 301 S. RIDGEWOOD AVENUE DAYTONA BEACH, FLORIDA 32114 PHONE (386) 671- 8178 FAX (386) 671- 8149 Dear Candidate: Campaign Signs are now governed by 6.10.J.12, Land Development Code, Temporary Signs and the applicable regulations are listed below. If you have any questions please contact Permits & Licensing at (386) 671- 8178. 1. Temporary signs may not be displayed on any parcel more than 100 days in any 12- month period. 2. In residential zoning districts, a maximum of 10 temporary signs, each sign not to exceed 3 square feet in sign area, may be displayed on each parcel of land. 3. In nonresidential zoning districts, temporary signs with total sign area not exceeding 32 square feet for each 100 feet of street frontage shall be permitted. 4. No sign shall be erected on public property or right -of way. 5. No sign permit will be required for these temporary signs. Candidates and property owners are strongly encouraged to timely remove the signs once the election is over and/ or the sign is no longer relevant.

THE CITY OF DAYTONA BEACH

Dear Candidate:
Campaign Signs are now governed by 6.10.J.12, Land Development Code, Temporary Signs and
the applicable regulations are listed below. If you have any questions please contact Permits &
Licensing at (386) 671- 8178.

1. Temporary signs may not be displayed on any parcel more than 100 days in any 12- month
period.
2. In residential zoning districts, a maximum of 10 temporary signs, each sign not to exceed 3 square feet in sign area, may be displayed on each parcel of land.

3. In nonresidential zoning districts, temporary signs with total sign area not exceeding 32 square feet for each 100 feet of street frontage shall be permitted.

4.
No sign shall be erected on public property or right -of way.

5. No sign permit will be required for these temporary signs.

Candidates and property owners are strongly encouraged to timely remove the signs once the
election is over and/ or the sign is no longer relevant.
