PEABODY AUDITORIUM ADVISORY BOARD
Overview

The Peabody Auditorium Advisory Board of Daytona Beach is a group of appointment members with a common goal of encouraging and promoting the initiatives and programs offered at the Peabody Auditorium.  

Meetings

4 p.m.
Second Tuesday of each month
Peabody Auditorium
600 Auditorium Blvd.
Daytona Beach, FL 32114

Meetings are open to the public. We invite you to attend and share your ideas, concerns and suggestions about how we can work together to improve our city.
