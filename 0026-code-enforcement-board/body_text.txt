CODE ENFORCEMENT BOARD
Overview

The Code Enforcement Board of Daytona Beach is an appointed board of citizens with a common goal to promote, protect and improve the health, safety and welfare of the citizens in our city.

Commission Chambers

Meetings are open to the public. We invite you to attend and share with us your ideas, concerns and suggestions about how we can work together to improve our city.
