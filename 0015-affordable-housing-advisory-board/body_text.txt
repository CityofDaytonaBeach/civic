AFFORDABLE HOUSING ADVISORY BOARD
Overview

The Affordable Housing Advisory Board (AHAB) of Daytona Beach is a volunteer board of citizens with a common goal to encourage and facilitate affordable housing while protecting the ability of the property to appreciate in value.
