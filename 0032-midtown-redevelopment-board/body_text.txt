MIDTOWN REDEVELOPMENT BOARD
Overview

The Midtown Redevelopment Board of Daytona Beach is a board of appointed members with a common goal to review and decide major site plans in the Midtown Redevelopment Area. 

Meetings
6 p.m.
Second Tuesday of each month
City Hall
Commission Chambers
301 S. Ridgewood St.
Daytona Beach, FL 32114

Meetings are open to the public. We invite you to attend and share with us your ideas, concerns and suggestions about how we can work together to improve our city.
