SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY

PURPOSE

This Policy sets forth guidelines for establishment and use by the Daytona Beach
Downtown Development Authority ( “DDA ”) of social media sites as a means of
conveying DDA information to the public or in otherwise promoting the DDA’s
goals and objectives.

For the purposes of this Policy:

commentary , including without limitation blogs, the DDA Web Page, wiki’s,
content hosting sites such as Flickr and YouTube, and social networking sites
such as Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter. This Policy is in addition to and
complements any existing or future DDA policies regarding the use of
technology, computers, smart phones, e -mail and the internet.

district of th e City.

that responsibility by the DDA governing board. The Content Manager is

2. Providing hyperl inks consistent with this Policy .
